About Us



Ekac Development NPO was established in 2016, the organisation's core functions are Agriculture, Soccer, Feeding Scheme and Social Issues. We have successfully been educating the youth of Meadowlands on how to plant their own food and giving back with the same food we planted, and we manage to give young boys a place of belonging in our soccer team and removing them from the streets where they would be exposed to drugs and all the negative things that affect young people. The Organisation has a positive impact in Meadowlands youth, their families and community at large.

Ekac Development is guided by a volonteer board of directors, dedicated volunteers and day to day coaching stuff. Our primary revenue source of income or donation is from the plants that we sell sometimes, and families who can afford to assist with all the funds we try to buy seeds and training equipment for the soccer team.





Our Vision



Ekac Development exist to bring massive change in the mindset of young people in terms of education and how they perceives poverty, into the knowledge that in every problem there is a solution.
